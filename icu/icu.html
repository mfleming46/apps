ICU (Interleaved Clicking Up)

Dr. Molly Gebrian’s Interleaved Clicking Up method is a powerful practice tool, but it can sometimes feel like navigating a maze. This app provides turn-by-turn guidance to keep you on the right path.

Start: 60   Target: 120   Step: 5

60 BPM
segs 1---2

This app uses cookies to remember your inputs between sessions — just on your device, nothing is tracked or shared.

Reference: Gebrian, Molly, Learn Faster, Perform Better: A Musician's Guide to the Neuroscience of Practicing, Oxford University Press, 2024


This app assumes you're already familiar with the ICU practice method. it's purpose is to tell you what tempo and segments to play next.

Segment Your Passage: Break your piece into short, manageable segments.

Set Your Practice Parameters: In the settings, choose your starting tempo, target tempo, and tempo increment.

Practice Progression: Click Next BPM to get the current segment and tempo to practice. Repeat as needed.

Automatic Tempo Adjustment: If using the built-in method, the app will automatically update the tempo for you.
