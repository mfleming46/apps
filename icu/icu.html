ICU (Interleaved Clicking Up)

Dr. Molly Gebrian’s Interleaved Clicking Up method can feel like navigating a maze. This app helps you stay on track. It tells you exactly what segment to play and sets the metronome for you, so you can focus on your musical goals.

BPMSegments

 

 

This app uses cookies to remember your inputs between sessions — just on your device, nothing is tracked or shared.

Reference: Gebrian, Molly, Learn Faster, Perform Better: A Musician's Guide to the Neuroscience of Practicing, Oxford University Press, 2024


Play the highlighted item, then tap Pass, Fail, or Skip.

Faster/Slower adjusts tempo and resets streak.

Blocked Mode: Stay on an item until you pass 5 times in a row.

Interleaved Mode: Move to the next after each try.

Streak = number of consecutive passes.

You can edit item names and optional BPMs.

Metronome Icon toggles audible metronome; Number dropdown selects metronome subdivision.
