Name that Note

Sharpen your note recognition skills. This quiz trains you to quickly identify clarinet notes across an extended range — from low E below the staff up to super‑high C.

The quiz focuses on the 17 notes above and below the staff. Each card shows you a note and asks you to name it. There are two versions: the Easy method gives you immediate feedback after every card. The Advanced approach shows your score only after you have completed the quiz.

Select your preferred mode to start the quiz.
